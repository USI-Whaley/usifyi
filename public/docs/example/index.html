    USI helpdesk quick links and help #

    Request a password reset/account activation #

    Password resets a USI must be done over the phone due to USI security policy. When you call in you will be asked to verify a couple of pieces of information confirm that you are who you say you are. Having your student ID number (usually starts with 000) or your username, which is the start of your USI email. The username for your myUSI account is always your USI email, not a gmail/yahoo/other email provider. If you are trying to activate/sign in for the first time.

    Multifactor reset/new phone #

    If you get yourself locked out of your account due to losing access to one of your multifacotr authetication methods, you’ll need to call the helpdesk to have it reset.

    If you are getting a new phone and still have your old phone, you can go here which has instructions on how to add your new phone to your account before you switch phones.
